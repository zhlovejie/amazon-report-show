# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Chinese-speaking Amazon marketplace operators who reconcile monthly sales, fees,
storage, advertising, landed cost, and profit data from seller reports.

## Product Purpose

Turn Amazon CSV and advertising statement data into an auditable SKU-level monthly
profit report, while making unmatched fees and product configuration gaps explicit.

## Brand Personality

Operational, precise, and calm. The interface should favor trustworthy calculations
and efficient repeated work over decorative presentation.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition, oversized display type, decorative dashboards,
and unfamiliar controls that slow down report reconciliation.

## Design Principles

- Keep source data, calculated values, and manual corrections distinguishable.
- Surface mismatches before calculations can silently lose or misattribute money.
- Use familiar table, form, and status patterns for repeated monthly workflows.
- Preserve user-entered configuration and provide reversible recovery actions.
- Keep dense financial information scannable without hiding important detail.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use WCAG 2.1 AA as the baseline, preserve keyboard access and visible focus states,
and never communicate validation or financial status through color alone.
